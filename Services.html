Emergency Care

Our 24/7 Emergency Department is equipped to handle critical situations such as heart attacks, severe injuries, and other life-threatening conditions. With a team of highly trained doctors and advanced medical equipment, we ensure timely and efficient care.

Surgical Services

We offer a wide range of surgical procedures, from routine operations to complex surgeries. Our state-of-the-art operating rooms are equipped with the latest technology, ensuring precision and safety. Our experienced surgeons specialize in general surgery, orthopedics, neurosurgery, and more.

Maternity and Neonatal Care

Our maternity department offers comprehensive care for expecting mothers and newborns. From prenatal check-ups to labor and delivery, we provide personalized care to ensure the health and well-being of both mother and baby. Our neonatal unit is equipped to handle premature and high-risk births.

Diagnostic Imaging

Our diagnostic imaging services, including X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds, allow for accurate and timely diagnosis of medical conditions. With advanced technology and skilled radiologists, we provide high-quality imaging for various diagnostic needs.

Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation

Our rehabilitation services focus on helping patients recover from surgery, injury, or illness. With personalized physical therapy plans, our team works to restore mobility, strength, and independence, ensuring a smooth recovery process tailored to individual needs.
