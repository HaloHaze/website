Welcome To Alliance Hospital

Welcome You

All we care is your health and well being hence, we, at Alliance Hospital treat all our patients with full care, assisting all their needs while ensuring their health and fitness. Here, patients have access to all medical and surgical sub-specialties within a single institution, empowering the medical team to address all the patient's health needs and treat the whole person.

Why Choose Us?

When it comes to your health, choosing the right healthcare provider is crucial. At our facility, we are dedicated to delivering an exceptional standard of care, backed by a team of over 130 highly skilled professionals. Our team includes a globally recognized panel of doctors who bring years of expertise and a deep commitment to providing personalized, patient-centered care. Every member of our team is passionate about your well-being, working collaboratively to ensure that you receive the highest level of medical attention tailored to your unique needs.

  • Expert Team: Over 130 professionals, including globally recognized doctors, dedicated to personalized patient care.
  • Advanced Technology: State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment for accurate and timely diagnoses.
  • Reliable Lab Services: Managed by Lanka Hospital Diagnostics (LHD) to ensure precise and dependable results.
  • Patient-Centered Care: Compassionate and respectful treatment with clear communication and support.
  • Comprehensive Services: A wide range of medical services under one roof for all your healthcare needs.

Departments of our Hospital

  1. Cardiology: Focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of heart and cardiovascular conditions.
  2. Orthopedics: Specializes in the treatment of the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments.
  3. Neurology: Deals with disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.
  4. Oncology: Provides care for patients with cancer, including diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up care.
  5. Pediatrics: Focuses on the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents.
